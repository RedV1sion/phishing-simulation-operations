# Executive Summary

During Q2 2026, the Security Operations Team conducted multiple enterprise phishing simulation campaigns to evaluate employee phishing susceptibility, improve organizational security awareness, and reduce phishing-related risk across the Microsoft 365 enterprise environment.

The phishing awareness program focused on realistic enterprise phishing scenarios including:

- Credential harvesting attacks
- IT impersonation phishing
- Business Email Compromise (BEC)
- HR social engineering
- Security breach notifications
- QR code phishing simulations

Overall phishing awareness metrics improved during the reporting period; however, elevated phishing susceptibility remains among remote workforce users, finance personnel, and repeat offenders.
