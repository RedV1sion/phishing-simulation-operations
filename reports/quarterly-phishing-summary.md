# Executive Summary

During Q2 2026, the Security Operations Team conducted multiple enterprise phishing simulation campaigns to evaluate employee phishing susceptibility, improve organizational security awareness, and reduce phishing-related risk across the Microsoft 365 enterprise environment.

The phishing operations program focused on:

- Credential harvesting simulations
- IT impersonation attacks
- Human Resources social engineering
- Financial fraud / BEC scenarios
- Data breach alert phishing
- User remediation and awareness training

Overall phishing awareness metrics improved during the reporting period; however, elevated phishing susceptibility remained among remote users, finance personnel, and repeat offenders.
