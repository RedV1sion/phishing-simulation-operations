# Purpose

The purpose of this remediation tracking report is to document remediation activities assigned to users who failed phishing simulations or demonstrated elevated phishing-related risk behaviors.

This report supports:

- User risk reduction
- Security awareness improvement
- Repeat offender monitoring
- Awareness program effectiveness tracking
- Enterprise phishing risk management

---

# Remediation Workflow Overview

The remediation process followed the workflow below:

1. User fails phishing simulation
2. User risk score updated
3. Automated remediation training assigned
4. Follow-up phishing simulation scheduled
5. Repeat offender review conducted
6. Escalation to management where necessary

# Remediation Categories

## 1. Credential Harvesting Failures

Users who submitted credentials during phishing simulations received additional training focused on:

- Credential harvesting attacks
- Microsoft 365 phishing threats
- MFA phishing scams
- Secure login verification
- URL inspection techniques

---

## 2. IT Impersonation Failures

Users who interacted with fake IT notifications received additional training focused on:

- MFA reset scams
- Security alert phishing
- Internal IT impersonation
- Account suspension phishing
- Verification of internal communications

---

## 3. HR Social Engineering Failures

Users who interacted with HR-themed phishing campaigns received additional training covering:

- Social engineering awareness
- HR impersonation attacks
- Internal policy phishing
- Employee portal phishing scams

---

## 4. Financial Scam Failures

Finance and administrative users who interacted with invoice/payment phishing campaigns received training related to:

- Business Email Compromise (BEC)
- Vendor impersonation attacks
- Invoice fraud
- Payment verification procedures
- Executive impersonation scams
